Our Story

About AZ BROZ Inc.

Two Friends. One Vision. The North.

AZ BROZ Inc. was born from a shared dream between two lifelong friends who saw a growing need for reliable, high-quality contracting services right here in the North. Having grown up understanding the unique challenges that come with living and working in Yellowknife and the surrounding Northwest Territories, they knew the North deserved better — better service, better craftsmanship, and a contractor who truly understood the demands of the northern environment.

What began as a small operation fuelled by passion, hard work, and northern grit quickly grew into a full-service contracting company trusted by homeowners and businesses alike. At AZ BROZ, we don't just complete projects — we build lasting relationships with our clients, our community, and the land we work on every day.

We take immense pride in being a northern company, by northerners, for northerners. Every challenge the North throws at us — permafrost, extreme temperatures, remote logistics — we face with experience, ingenuity, and an unrelenting drive to get the job done right. That's the AZ BROZ promise.
